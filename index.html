Our Work

We know how often phone screens get shattered after a night out at Bents. Our student-run business understands the struggle of fixing a phone screen; the long wait times, the overpriced repairs, and the unnecessary fees.

The goal of iFix UMD is to bring a significantly cheaper alternative to many of the iPhone repair shops around campus without sacrificing quality. We specialize in iPhone screen repairs. Our experience has been built from fixing screens and other iPhones parts for the past 3 years.

Our Promise

Sometimes, parts don't always work as expected. Maybe we messed something up, or maybe the actual piece is broken. Whatever the case may be, if anything is wrong with the part you paid for. You have up to 2 weeks to let us know and come to us with your phone. We'll check it out, and if we need to, we'll replace the part free of charge.

You will have to manage with the broken part for a few days while a replacement comes in, but we will reinstall the new one as soon as we can.

NOTE: When replacing a part that was paid for, the part must be in an almost-new condition, or you will not be able to return it.
